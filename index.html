About

What is Thread?

Thread, a bed set including a pillow case, sheet and optional eye mask, is designed to help people sleep better and connect people across the word. Thread has sensor that can detect your body temperature, track your sleep cycle and wake you up at the optimal time. However, we believe the power of Thread comes from connecting with others, not just looking at your own data. With Thread you can connect with people across the world who have similar sleeping patterns as you. Thread is also connected to a dream diary where you can make notes about your good, bad or neutral dreams and easily share your stories with people around the world. When possible, we integrate dreams you logged with the last REM cycle we captured for that night.

Features

See facts about your own sleep health, thread will wake you up at the optimal time.

Connect with another person in another part of the world who shared your same sleep pattern.

Understand patterns of sleep in different parts of the entire world.

Share and discover dreams experienced during sleep in our community.

Our Missions

Our mission is to help the entire world sleep better and connect people across the world by sharing their most intimate experiences. We aim to create a community that empowers individuals with empathy and understanding by sharing sleep patterns, dreams and experiences. We believe that technology has the potential for making people feel connected and creating a friendly community.

Additionally, for every thread set bought, we will donate one to someone in the opposite timezone, to create more opportunities for people across the world from each other to connect.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do you offer different sizes?chevron_right

    Thread comes in Twin, Queen and King sizes. However, if you have another size in mind, just ask! Our technology is flexible and is more about our thread based sensors than the sheets themselves.

  • chevron_rightHow does Thread work?

    Thread using discrete microlocation and connected scale devices to help track your sleep patterns and behaviors, much as you may already have on an app on your phone, but much more accurate since it is incorporated into your sheets and pillow.

  • chevron_rightHow can I control who sees my information?

    After connecting the bed set with your device, you can opt in to share your name and information. Your data will be mapped anonymously if you keep your information private.
